About Me

I’m Gautam Chandna, a founder and product generalist focused on regulated and trust-driven spaces. I lead Skiwo as CEO, where we build infrastructure for interpretation, background checks, and other services used by governments and institutions.

We started with TikkTalk, a marketplace for certified interpreters, which later merged into Salita in 2020. Since then, we’ve broadened our focus—from language access to tools that support safety, compliance, and operational integrity across multiple industries via Manymore.com and Payout Partner.

I’m also on the board of Cake It Easy, investor (ex-board member) at Diffia, and I volunteer as an advisor to early-stage impact startups.
